About Ajira Digital Club

We are a student-led organization dedicated to equipping members with digital skills for the modern workforce.

Our Mission

To bridge the digital skills gap among students by providing training, mentorship, and resources for online work opportunities.

Our Vision

A future where every student has access to digital skills training and can leverage online opportunities for economic empowerment.

Our Goals

  • Train 500+ students annually
  • Create 200+ digital work profiles
  • Facilitate 50+ online jobs

Our Services & Activities

We offer comprehensive programs to help students develop digital skills and connect with opportunities.

Digital Training

Comprehensive courses in digital marketing, programming, graphic design, and more.

  • Hands-on practical sessions
  • Certification upon completion

Mentorship

  • One-on-one mentorship
  • Career guidance
  • Portfolio reviews

Freelancing

  • Job search strategies
  • Payment management
